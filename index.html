    ABOUT SHARON

    Vancouver based Graphic and Web designer Chung Man (Sharon) Tam is a stylish girl who makes stylish things. She left Hong Kong for Canada at the age of 16 and took the Graphic Design program, the New Media and Web development program and Digital Design and Development program at British Columbia Institute of Technology in Vancouver after graduating high school.

    As an international student and traveller, Sharon creates designs with an ability to combine experience and knowledge to design multicultural and passionate work.

    Feel Free to contact her and give her a job. Got no problem with Chinese and English. She is bilingual and multicultural, hence, a bit more resourceful and open minded. If you want to know more about Sharon, please check out her portfolio.
